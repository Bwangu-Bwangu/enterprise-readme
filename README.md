## 🧭 Who We Are

**Bwangubwangu ICT Consultancy** is a Zambian-based ICT firm dedicated to helping organizations digitally transform through innovative, scalable, and sustainable technology solutions.

We specialize in:
- IT Infrastructure & Support
- Web and App Development
- Cybersecurity Services
- Cloud & Hosting Solutions
- Digital Strategy and Transformation
